A Cat Named JARV

JARV is an MC from Vermont who has been racking in hundreds of thousand of listeners on his last few songs. His lyrical flow and beats take inspiration from all walks of music, sampling jazz, hip-hop, and even rock. JARV is expected to have a breakout within the next few years, showing passion, determination, and genius in his music.



In his song "FUNKY DRUMMER" Jarv embodies his style and flow nearly perfectly. Jarv doesn't take himself too seriously however, he knows he's more than just "any average cat." Jarv samples a fast paced lead and adds his drum skills in to create the instumental to this song. Only then does he compliment such music with his lyrical flow on how hes the funkiest drummer around.
